ArtVista Exhibition 2026

About the exhibition

ArtVista Exhibition 2025 showcases contemporary art from emerging local artists. The exhibition features over 50 stunning artworks including paintings, sculptures, and digital art. Our mission is to promote local talent and provide a platform for artists to connect with art enthusiasts. The exhibition runs from March 15 to April 30, 2025, at the City Arts Center. Entry is free for students and seniors. Join us for an inspiring journey through creativity and innovation.

The exhibition space spans over 5,000 square feet across three beautifully designed galleries, each offering a unique atmosphere and theme. Gallery One focuses on traditional paintings and portraiture, Gallery Two showcases three-dimensional sculptures and installations, while Gallery Three is dedicated to cutting-edge digital and mixed media artworks. Our climate-controlled environment ensures the preservation and optimal presentation of each piece, with professional lighting that brings out the depth, texture, and vibrant colors of every artwork.

Our Mission

To celebrate local artistic talent by providing an accessible platform for emerging artists to showcase their work. We foster creativity, encourage artistic dialogue, and build a vibrant arts community that connects creators with art enthusiasts and the general public.

Our Vission

To become the Northern Province's premier destination for contemporary art, recognized as a catalyst for cultural enrichment and artistic innovation. We aim to establish ArtVista as an annual tradition that celebrates artistic diversity and contributes to Sri Lanka's creative economy.
